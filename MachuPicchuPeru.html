🏔 Machu Picchu: Lost City of the Incas

Home Great Wall Taj Mahal

Machu Picchu, nestled high in the Andes Mountains, is a breathtaking citadel of the Inca Empire. Hidden for centuries, it was rediscovered in 1911 and remains one of the most mysterious and awe-inspiring sites on Earth.

📜 Quick Facts

  • 📍 Location: Peru
  • 🏗 Built: 15th century
  • ⛰ Altitude: 2,430 m (7,970 ft)
  • ✨ UNESCO World Heritage Site

✨ Highlights

Sun Gate

Offers a panoramic view of the citadel at sunrise.

Temple of the Sun

A circular tower with astronomical significance.

Terraces

Ingenious farming terraces carved into the mountainside.
