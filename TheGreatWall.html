🏯 The Great Wall of China

Home Chichen Itza Machu Picchu

The Great Wall of China stretches over 21,000 kilometers across mountains and deserts. Built over centuries, it served as protection against invasions and is one of the most iconic landmarks of history.

📜 Quick Facts

  • 📍 Location: Northern China
  • 🏗 Built: 7th century BC - 17th century AD
  • 🌍 Length: 21,196 km
  • ✨ UNESCO World Heritage Site

✨ Highlights

Badaling Section

The most visited part, restored and tourist-friendly.

Mutianyu

Less crowded, with breathtaking mountain views.

Jiankou

Wild and unrestored section for adventure seekers.
