The Colosseum, also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, is an iconic symbol of the Roman Empire. Located in the heart of Rome, it once hosted gladiator battles, animal hunts, and public spectacles. With its massive stone arches and innovative engineering, the Colosseum remains one of the most remarkable architectural achievements of ancient times.
